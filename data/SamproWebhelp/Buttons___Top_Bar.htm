Top Bar Buttons

Top Bar buttons are located along the top of your application windows where they are accessible at all times. The Top Bar is scrollable; some of the buttons listed below are available by scrolling the button bar down. Top Bar buttons are used to perform functions such as accessing the viewers list and folders screen, printing reports, and so on. Tab-style buttons located only on a specific screen (e.g., the GEN tab in the Purchase Order Viewer) are discussed in the context specific help for that viewer.

To view additional information on a button within the application, position the mouse cursor over the button and then click the right mouse button. To view more detailed help file information on a button, simply click o its description below.
